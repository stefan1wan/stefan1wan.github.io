I am a PHD student at [Purdue University](https://www.purdue.edu) working with Prof. [Dave Tian](https://davejingtian.org) since May 2023. My research interests span computer security and systems. Recently, I have been focusing on topics related to GPUs, Rowhammer, and side-channel attacks. From August 2025 to March 2026, I was a visiting student at Clemson University, where I worked with Prof. [Zhenkai Zhang](https://zhenkai-zhang.github.io/) on GPU Rowhammer, including GeForge. Before PHD, I earned both my Bachelor and Master degree from  Fudan University, working with Prof. [Zhe Zhou](https://www.y-droid.com/zhe/index.html). Additionally, I was a member of Fudan's CTF team [\*\*\*\*\*\*(Sixstars)](https://github.com/sixstars).
